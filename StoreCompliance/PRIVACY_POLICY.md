Final Number Studios ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile game, Final Number.

- **Gameplay Data**: Game progress, scores, achievements, and preferences
- **Feedback**: Any feedback or support requests you submit

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the game, session duration, feature usage
- **Crash Reports**: Technical information when the game crashes or has errors

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:
- Provide and maintain the game
- Improve user experience and game features
- Monitor game performance and fix technical issues
- Comply with legal obligations
- Send non-marketing communications (service updates, security alerts)

## Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process personal data based on:
- **Performance of a Contract**: Providing the game services
- **Legitimate Interests**: Improving the game, fraud prevention, network security
- **Legal Obligation**: Compliance with applicable laws
- **Consent**: For advertising personalization (can be withdrawn anytime)

## Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- **Access**: Request a copy of your personal data
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of your personal data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
- **Restriction**: Limit how we use your data
- **Objection**: Object to certain types of processing
- **Withdraw Consent**: Withdraw previously given consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@finalnumber.example.com

## Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for:
- Providing the game services
- Compliance with legal obligations

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:
